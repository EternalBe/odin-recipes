Super Sanwhich

Description

The ideal sandhwhich. A 16-inch sub pack full of all the ingredients that get your body going. The aroma is tantalizing. Best served with a side of chips and a soda.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • Onions
  • Smoked Turkey
  • Vinegar
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Oregano
  • Mayonaise

Steps

  1. Cut your bread open
  2. Layer your turkey slices
  3. layer your cheese slices on the turkey
  4. slap all the veggies on, then add oil, vinegar, salt, and oregano on top
  5. Apply mayonaise to the alternate side of the bread
  6. Optional: Prepare dill pickles to crunch on on the side in between bite of your sandwhich
